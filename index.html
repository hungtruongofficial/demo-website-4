welcome

to Our Salon

where magic comes true!

You’re looking for a quick polish change, a relaxing spa pedicure? Wanna impressive waxing or perfect eyelashes? Come to Our Salon and experience upscale services while enjoying the luxurious ambience.

Salon’s mission is to use only natural and eco-friendly products and services that allow our customers to look their best while promoting health and overall wellbeing. Our diverse professionals with many years of experience will dedicate to providing you with one-of-a-kind services and catering to your every need.

Manicure

Fingernails and hands are pampered with clean & quality tools to improve the look and feel.

Regular Manicure
$20

Soak your hands in warm soapy water to soothe and soften dead skin cells, cuticle treatment, hand massage with cream. Then finish with polish for the complete look.

Spa Manicure
$25

After taking care of nails and cuticles, we’ll finish up with a warm hydrating massage, and beautifully polished nails.

Signature Manicure
$40

All steps in Spa Manicure plus light sugar scrub with essential oils, an extra mineral mask, hot towel, and longer massage to ease muscle tension and reduce pain. Polished with precision to complete the total treat.
